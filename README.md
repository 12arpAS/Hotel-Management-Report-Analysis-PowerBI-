# 🏨 Hotel Management Report: KPI & Business Intelligence Analysis
📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing hotel booking data using Business Intelligence techniques to uncover valuable insights and improve decision-making in the hospitality industry. Built using Microsoft Power BI, Excel, and DAX, the report converts raw data into interactive dashboards that provide a 360-degree analytical view of hotel operations.

The dataset includes booking information for both City Hotel and Resort Hotel, allowing comparative analysis across different business segments. The project highlights key operational issues such as a 37% cancellation rate, resulting in a 4.50 million revenue loss, and proposes data-driven solutions.

# 🎯 Business Problem

The hospitality industry often struggles with:

High booking cancellations
Revenue leakage
Poor customer retention
Seasonal demand fluctuations
Over-dependence on third-party booking platforms

This project aims to solve these problems using data-driven insights and visualization.

# 🔍 Key Insights & Findings
A 37% cancellation rate significantly reduces profitability
Online Travel Agencies (OTAs) contribute 53% of revenue, but also drive the highest cancellations
Peak demand occurs in July–August, impacting pricing and occupancy
Low repeat customer rate indicates weak customer loyalty
City Hotels experience higher cancellations compared to Resort Hotels
# 💡 Business Recommendations
Implement dynamic pricing strategies during peak seasons
Introduce loyalty programs to improve customer retention
Optimize booking policies to reduce cancellations
Improve operational processes in City Hotels
Encourage direct bookings to reduce dependency on OTAs
